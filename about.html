Hey, I'm Henry. I'm a photographer based in Arizona and Houston, focusing on portraits, outdoor editorial work, and storytelling through natural light and clean composition. This site is a growing collection of some of my favorite projects and experiments.

I shoot both digital and film, and I'm always looking for new environments, textures, and light to work with. When I'm not shooting, I'm usually biking, backpacking, or digging into engineering projects.
