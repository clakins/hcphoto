About the Project

The 'Sunburn' series explores the fundamental connection between light, time, and the photographic process. By using large-format, custom-built cameras as solar telescopes, the paper—a direct positive photographic material—is exposed for long periods, sometimes an entire day.

The sun's intensity burns and scorches the paper, leaving a visible path of light recorded through time, resulting in unique, one-of-a-kind prints that are both image and object.
